About Me

I am Thuto Molao, an Interior Designer. I enjoy taking complex problems and turning them into simple beautiful designs. I love the logic and structure around interior designing. I pride myself in giving clients an elegant outcome beyond my visualization skills.

I am an excellent problem solver, and I understand the importance of being able to work autonomously in a fast-paced environment. Whilst dedicating my commitment to clients, I see myself as a well-rounded individual with the devotion necessary to succeed as an Interior Designer.
